Aaron Ashear, M.S., PPC

(he/him/his)

I aim to provide a safe environment for you to process life events, stressors, and challenges that prevent you from fully flourishing in life. I will work together with you as a supportive and non-judgemental partner. Together, we will help you develop greater clarity and hope in the areas you think are important. Much of my work has been with emerging adults, particularly around issues of anxiety, motivation, depression, masculinity, LGBTQ issues, and general decision making.

I will help you define your own values and develop your own goals, and we will develop the tools to help you move towards lasting and significant change in areas you deem important. I blend principles from humanistic, cognitive behavioral, and mindfulness-based modalities. You are the expert of your life and situation that brings you to counseling. I am not here to fix you. Thank you for considering me as your counselor and I wish you health, contentment, and connection.

I hold a Provisional Professional in Counseling License through the State of Wyoming, and I am an intern under the supervision of Dr. Fitzgerald. I practice at Centerpoint in Laramie, WY. Please contact me to make an appointment, or with any questions.
