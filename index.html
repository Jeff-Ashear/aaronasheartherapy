Credentials

I hold a Master’s of Science in Mental Health Counseling from the University of Wyoming. I also hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology from the University of Wyoming. As a provisionally licensed counselor, I work under supervision of Dr. Anne Fitzgerald at Centerpoint Counseling in Laramie,Wyoming. Anne is both the director of Centerpoint Counseling and a licensed clinical psychologist. She earned her PhD in clinical psychology from the University of Wyoming. Her license is: Wyoming #308, and has been practicing professionally since 1994.


Philosophy of Counseling

My goal is to provide a safe, confidential environment in which clients are provided the space to process life events, stressors and challenges that may be inhibiting contentment with their life and lifestyle. In counseling, the collaboration between counselor and client is extremely important and should be nurtured and maintained. I strive to make the counseling process supportive, non-judgemental, and mutually proactive to develop greater clarity and hope in areas the client seeks counsel on. With the tools provided, you define their own values, develop their own goals, and move towards lasting and significant change in areas they deem important. You are the expert of your life and situation that brings you to counseling. I honor this while at the same time expect you to take an active role in the therapeutic relationship and process.
