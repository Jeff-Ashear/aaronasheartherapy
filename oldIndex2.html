Credentials

I hold a Master’s of Science in Mental Health Counseling from the University of Wyoming. I also hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology from the University of Wyoming. As a provisionally licensed counselor, I work under supervision of Dr. Anne Fitzgerald at Centerpoint Counseling in Laramie,Wyoming. Anne is both the director of Centerpoint Counseling and a licensed clinical psychologist. She earned her PhD in clinical psychology from the University of Wyoming. Her license is: Wyoming #308, and has been practicing professionally since 1994. You can learn more about Centerpoint Counseling here.
